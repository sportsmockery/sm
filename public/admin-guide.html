‚úçÔ∏è Writing & Publishing Posts

Creating a New Post

Go to Admin Dashboard

Navigate to sportsmockery.com/admin and log in with your credentials.

Click "New Post"

Find the "New Post" button on the dashboard or in the sidebar under Posts.

Write Your Article

Enter your title (slug auto-generates). Use the editor toolbar for formatting: bold, italic, links, images, headings, lists, quotes.

Set Category & Featured Image

In the right sidebar, select the team category (Bears, Cubs, etc.) and upload a featured image.

Add SEO Info

Fill in SEO title and meta description. These appear in Google search results.

Publish or Save Draft

Click "Publish" to go live immediately, or "Save Draft" to continue later.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Where did my old posts go?
All 31,096 posts were migrated! They're at the same URLs they were before. The only change is faster loading.
Why does /wp-admin not work anymore?
We're no longer on WordPress. The new admin is at /admin. Same login credentials work.
Can I still use Yoast for SEO?
Yoast was a WordPress plugin. SEO is now built-in! Fill out the SEO Title and Meta Description fields in the post editor sidebar.
Where are my images?
All images are still hosted at the same URLs on the old server. They load through our CDN for speed. Upload new images through the Media Library in admin.
How do I embed tweets/videos?
Paste the Twitter or YouTube URL directly into the editor on its own line. It will auto-embed.
What happened to Disqus comments?
Disqus still works! All existing comments are preserved. The same Disqus thread loads on each article.
Why is the site so fast now?
Next.js pre-renders pages, Vercel serves from edge locations worldwide, and images are auto-optimized. No more WordPress plugin bloat!
Can I schedule posts for later?
Yes! In the Publish panel, change the date/time before clicking Publish. The post will go live at that time.
Where does the AI mockery come from?
We use Claude AI (by Anthropic) to generate witty predictions and commentary. It's integrated throughout the site for entertainment value.
Who do I contact for help?
Reach out to Chris (founder) for technical issues. For editorial questions, contact Erik (editor).
