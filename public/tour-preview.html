GM Trade Simulator
Think you can do better than your team's GM?

🚀 How It Works

Pick your Chicago team, then build a trade with any other team in their league. Our AI (powered by Claude) will grade your trade and tell you if it would actually happen!

📋 Steps:

  • 1️⃣ Pick your Chicago team (Bears, Bulls, Cubs, Sox, or Hawks)
  • 2️⃣ Select players you want to trade away
  • 3️⃣ Pick the other team you want to trade with
  • 4️⃣ Select players you want to get back
  • 5️⃣ Add draft picks if you want
  • 6️⃣ Submit and get your AI grade!

🎯 Grade Scale

75-100
ACCEPTED!
50-74
REJECTED
0-49
NO WAY!

🛑 Special Rules

  • Caleb Williams (Bears) = UNTOUCHABLE! Grade 0 if traded
  • Connor Bedard (Blackhawks) = UNTOUCHABLE! Grade 0 if traded
  • Limit: 10 trades per minute
  • Must be logged in to use

🏆 Leaderboard

Your grades add up! Compete with other fans to see who's the best GM!

Mock Draft Simulator
Pick your team's future stars!

🍿 What Is It?

It's draft day and YOU'RE in charge! Pick prospects for your Chicago team and see how the AI grades your draft class.

🏃 How to Play:

  • 🟢 Pick your team (if their draft window is open)
  • ⏩ When it's your pick, choose a prospect
  • ⏹️ Hit "Auto-Advance" to skip other teams' picks
  • 📚 Get your final draft grade from AI!

📖 Prospect Info

Each prospect shows you:

  • 👤 Name, position, and school
  • ⭐ Scouting grade (how good they are)
  • 💪 Strengths and weaknesses
  • 👥 Pro comparison (who they play like)

📅 When Can I Draft?

  • Each sport has a draft window
  • Teams must be eligible (not in playoffs, etc.)
  • Check the team selector to see who's ready!
