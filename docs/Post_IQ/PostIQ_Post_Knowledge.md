### Editorial Voice Characteristics

The analysis of 50+ articles reveals five core voice pillars:

1. **Passionate & Unfiltered** - Direct language, strategic profanity, emotional honesty
   - Example: "Let's not sugarcoat. For ten weeks, the Chicago Bears were bullying people." [sportsmockery](https://www.sportsmockery.com/chicago-bears/the-bears-run-game-face-planted-when-it-mattered-most-a-brutally-honest-autopsy/)

2. **Fan-First Perspective** - Uses "we/us" when discussing Chicago teams
   - Example: "We beat the Packers. We came back from 18 down." [sportsmockery](https://www.sportsmockery.com/chicago-bears/the-greatest-game-ever-played-inside-the-18-point-miracle-that-broke-green-bay/)

3. **Analytical But Accessible** - Complex stats presented conversationally
   - Blends EPA/play metrics with phrases like "pure, uncut cocaine for Bears fans" [sportsmockery](https://www.sportsmockery.com/chicago-bears/the-greatest-game-ever-played-inside-the-18-point-miracle-that-broke-green-bay/)

4. **Sarcastic & Self-Aware** - Chicago sports humor, acknowledges past failures
   - Frequent references to "Same Old Bears" narrative [sportsmockery](https://www.sportsmockery.com/chicago-bears/the-greatest-game-ever-played-inside-the-18-point-miracle-that-broke-green-bay/)

5. **Urgent & Immediate** - Present tense, breaking news energy even for analysis

### Article Structure Patterns

**Standard Template:**
1. **Opening Hook** (1-2 paragraphs) - Establish stakes and emotion
2. **Context** (1-2 paragraphs) - Background for casual fans
3. **Main Analysis** (3-7 sections) - Bold subheaders, data + narrative
4. **BFR YouTube Embed** - Standard placement after opening section
5. **Conclusion** - Forward-looking, team-specific sign-off ("Bear Down")

**Formatting Best Practices:**
- **Short paragraphs** (2-4 sentences typically)
- **Bold subheaders** every 3-4 paragraphs
- **Bold key statistics** and player names for scannability
- **Italics** for emphasis and sarcastic asides
- **Tables** for comparative data
- **Lists** for quick-hit information
