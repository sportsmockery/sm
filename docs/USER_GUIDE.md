# SportsMockery User Guide

Welcome to SportsMockery - your Bears-first Chicago sports destination.

## Navigation

### Main Header
The site features a sticky header with quick access to all Chicago teams:
- **HOME** - Return to the homepage
- **BEARS** - Chicago Bears coverage (our primary focus)
- **BULLS** - Chicago Bulls coverage
- **CUBS** - Chicago Cubs coverage
- **WHITE SOX** - Chicago White Sox coverage
- **BLACKHAWKS** - Chicago Blackhawks coverage

### Bears Sticky Bar
Below the main navigation, you'll see the Bears quick-access bar featuring:
- Current Bears record
- Next game information
- Quick links: News, Data Hub, Rumors, Podcasts
- "Get Bears Alerts" button for notifications

## Homepage Features

### Hero Carousel
The top of the homepage features a rotating carousel of the 5 most important stories, with Bears content prioritized. You can:
- Click the arrows to navigate manually
- Click the thumbnail images below to jump to specific stories
- Hover over the carousel to pause auto-rotation

### Team Spotlight
Expandable cards for each Chicago team showing:
- Current record and standings
- Latest articles from each team
- Quick stats at a glance

Bears is expanded by default and always appears first.

### For You Feed
A personalized article feed based on your favorite teams. Features include:
- Team filter buttons to focus on specific teams
- Star indicators for your favorite teams
- Chronological ordering within team priorities

### Timeline
A chronological view of all updates across Chicago sports:
- Breaking news is highlighted in red
- Bears stories have orange accents
- Load more to see older content

## Bears Hub (`/bears`)

The dedicated Bears section includes:

### Season Card
- Full season record
- Win percentage
- Current division standing
- Next and last game details
- Quick links to roster, stats, standings

### Key Players
Top Bears players with position-specific stats. Click any player to see their full profile.

### Trending Topics
Hot Bears storylines and topics being discussed. Topics marked "Hot" are especially active.

### Ask Bears AI
Get quick answers about the Bears with suggested questions or type your own.

## Personalization

### Favorite Teams
You can personalize your experience by selecting your favorite Chicago teams:
1. Bears is your default favorite (Bears-first design)
2. Add additional teams via your profile settings
3. Content from favorite teams appears first in feeds

### Notification Preferences
Control what alerts you receive:
- Breaking news
- Game alerts
- Weekly digest
- Trade rumors

## Reading Articles

### Article Features
- Reading time estimate at the top
- Table of contents for long articles (click to jump to sections)
- "From This Writer" sidebar showing other articles by the same author
- Related articles from the same team
- Social sharing buttons

### Saving Articles
Bookmark articles to read later (requires account).

## Search

Use the search icon in the header to find:
- Articles by keyword
- Specific topics
- Player names
- Historical coverage

## Dark Mode

Toggle between light and dark mode using the sun/moon icon in the header. Your preference is saved automatically.

## Mobile Experience

SportsMockery is fully responsive:
- Swipe through the hero carousel
- Tap the hamburger menu for navigation
- Tap team spotlight cards to expand
- Pull to refresh on article lists

## Accessibility

- Skip to content link for keyboard navigation
- All images have descriptive alt text
- Focus indicators for keyboard users
- High contrast color combinations
